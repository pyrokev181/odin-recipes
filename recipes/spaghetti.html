Spaghetti

Descrpition

This recipe is for a delicious American version of the Italian spaghetti alla bolognese. A very simple version of the dish, this recipes only uses 6 ingredients and can be easily adjusted to feed any amount of hungry people!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed tomato soup
  • 2 1/2 cups water

Steps:

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook ground beef with onion until meat is brown. Drain.
  3. Return meat mixture to pan with tomato paste, tomato soup, and water. Cook, stirring, until heated through and thickened, 5 to 15 minutes. Serve over cooked pasta.
